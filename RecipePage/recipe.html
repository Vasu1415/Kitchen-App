Chicken and Rice

30 Minute Meal Cooking Level 2 Contains Meat
×

1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and your choice of spices. 2. In a skillet, brown the chicken on both sides, then set aside. 3. Pour in chicken broth and place the chicken on top. 4. Bring to a boil, then simmer covered until rice is tender. 5. Stir in canned beans (drained and rinsed) during the last 5 minutes. 6. Serve hot.
